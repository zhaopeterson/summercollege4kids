Here are the instructors and their short bios.

Cheryl Brewer

Mrs. Brewer joined the program in 2008 with 35 years of teaching experience in business education in the East Side Union High School District in San Jose. She holds a B.S. in Social Science/Business and a Secondary Teaching Credential from San Jose State. In addition to her classroom experience, she was a school site department chairperson, STAR Testing Coordinator, Service Learning Coordinator, and an adult education instructor. At the district level, she assisted in applying for and implementing federal grants and monitored the district's school sites on their compliance requirements. Mrs. Brewer enjoys the challenge of offering a valuable educational experience for young people and strives to create a well-rounded curriculum for the program each year.

A Message from the Director

Dear Parents/Students:

As College 4 Kids enters its 21st year, we are looking forward to another great summer. The 2012 program will offer a broad range of courses that will challenge students in academic areas, while also offering creative and interesting elective courses in art, business, drama, music and sports. Two three-week sessions will be offered for students entering 6th through 9th grades in the fall.

Our 2012 schedule of classes, course descriptions, and registration information will be available by early February. All classes will be taught by credentialed and/or highly trained instructors on the West Valley College campus in Saratoga. Students will have the benefit of attending classes on a college campus where they can become acquainted with the college experience.

Our staff and instructors provide a well-supervised and safe environment in which to learn. We look forward to an exciting and enjoyable summer and hope you will join us for an enriching experience.

Cheryl Brewer, Program Director
